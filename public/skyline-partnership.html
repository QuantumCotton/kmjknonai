Proposal Overview

The Elite Service Hub is a performance-based lead generation firm specializing in kitchen and bathroom renovations. We are expanding into the Phoenix market and seek to establish a partnership with one premium contractor capable of delivering exceptional craftsmanship.

After reviewing your bathroom remodel work, we believe Skyline Group Construction represents the caliber of partner we require. This document outlines the proposed partnership structure.

Partnership Structure

The Elite Service Hub operates on a commission-based model, similar to a sales representative earning commission on closed deals. We charge 15% of the total project value for each completed job.

Our role is to generate qualified leads, convert them into signed contracts, and deliver those contracts to you for execution. You focus entirely on delivering exceptional work—we handle all lead generation and initial client acquisition.

Payment Terms

The 15% commission is structured as a two-payment system that aligns with standard contractor payment schedules:

Payment #1: Contract Signing

Amount: Approximately $2,500-$3,000 per job (estimated acquisition cost)

Timing: Due after you receive your first check from the customer

Integration: This cost is included within the labor portion of your bid to the customer. It represents the marketing investment required to acquire the lead.

Payment #2: Job Completion

Amount: Remaining commission balance (15% total commission minus Payment #1)

Timing: Due after you receive your final payment from the customer

Calculation Example: On a $55,000 project: ($55,000 × 15%) - $2,797 = $5,453 balance due

Key Advantage: You only pay The Elite Service Hub after you have been paid by the customer. There is no upfront cost or financial risk to your business.

Financial Breakdown: $55,000 Kitchen Project

Your Bid to Customer:

Materials + Labor (including marketing): $39,286
+ Your 40% Markup: $15,714
= Total Price to Customer: $55,000

Payment to The Elite Service Hub:

Payment #1 (after first customer check): $2,797
Payment #2 (after final customer check): $5,453
Total Commission (15% of $55,000): $8,250

Your Net Profit:

Gross Markup: $15,714
- Commission Balance (Payment #2): $5,453
= Your Net Profit: $10,261

Operational Process

Step 1: We generate qualified leads through our advertising channels.

Step 2: When a lead is closed, you execute the project and receive payment from the customer.

Step 3: You remit Payment #1 to The Elite Service Hub after receiving your first customer check.

Step 4: Upon project completion and final customer payment, you remit Payment #2 to The Elite Service Hub.

Step 5: We continue delivering additional leads as you complete each project, maintaining consistent workflow.

Timeline: It typically requires 60-90 days to fully stabilize a new market, optimize lead flow, and align with your team's capacity.
