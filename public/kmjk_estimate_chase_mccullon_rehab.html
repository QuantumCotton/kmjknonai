Project Overview

Complete interior and exterior rehabilitation of the clientâ€™s mobile home, including creation of a third bedroom and bathroom, full flooring replacement, structural panel upgrades, and finish improvements throughout. Work also includes exterior cleaning and roof coating to prepare the residence for market-ready presentation. AC equipment is to be supplied by the client and is outside of this scope. The effort is organized into a four-phase delivery plan so progress draws align with tangible milestone completions.

Scope of Work

  • Layout & Framing: Build out third bedroom and bathroom, expand small room footprint, repair or replace interior wall panels as needed.
  • Flooring & Trim: Remove existing floor coverings, install new subfloor repairs where required, lay new flooring throughout, and install new baseboards.
  • Kitchen Assembly: Reconfigure kitchen layout, assemble cabinetry and finishes, and install a compact island with client-approved footprint.
  • Bathroom Upgrade: Replace toilet, convert tub to standing shower with updated surround, ensure plumbing tie-ins and fixture alignment.
  • Exterior Cleaning: Power wash roof and exterior surfaces, apply protective roof coating, and wash/pain interior surfaces as specified.
  • Finishing & Cleanup: Patch, paint, and finish walls; remove debris daily; deliver final cleaning for client walk-through.
