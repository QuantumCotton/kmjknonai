Lead Generation Partnership Agreement

The Elite Service Hub's Agreement with KMJK

Agreement Overview

This Agreement outlines the partnership between The Elite Service Hub ("Lead Provider") and KMJK Home Improvement ("Contractor") for the provision of qualified kitchen renovation leads and the associated payment structure.

The Elite Service Hub will provide pre-qualified, ready-to-close leads to KMJK. KMJK agrees to include The Elite Service Hub's lead acquisition costs in their project bids and compensate The Elite Service Hub according to the payment structure outlined below.

Two-Payment Structure

📋 Check #1: Contract Signing (Customer to KMJK)

What's Included: Materials + Labor + Advertising Costs

Fixed Advertising Cost: $2,797 per job

Payment Flow: Customer pays KMJK upfront deposit. KMJK then pays The Elite Service Hub the $2,797 advertising cost.

This advertising cost is disclosed to KMJK and must be included in the project bid to the customer.

✅ Check #2: Job Completion (KMJK to The Elite Service Hub)

What's Included: The Elite Service Hub Commission Balance (15% Total - $2,797 Already Paid)

Calculation: (Project Value × 15%) - $2,797

Example on $30,000 job: ($30,000 × 15% = $4,500) - $2,797 = $1,703

Payment Flow: Upon job completion, KMJK pays The Elite Service Hub the remaining commission balance from their markup.

Key Agreement Terms

KMJK Home Improvement Obligations (Contractor):

  • Include the fixed $2,797 advertising cost in Materials + Labor + Advertising line item of customer bid
  • Pay The Elite Service Hub $2,797 upon contract signing (Check #1)
  • Pay The Elite Service Hub the commission balance (15% of total - $2,797) upon job completion (Check #2)
  • Maintain professional standards and quality workmanship on all projects
  • Complete projects within agreed-upon timelines

The Elite Service Hub Obligations (Lead Provider):

  • Provide pre-qualified, ready-to-close kitchen renovation leads to KMJK
  • Handle all marketing, advertising, and lead generation costs
  • Manage initial customer communications and qualification process
  • Close deals before introducing customer to KMJK
  • Provide ongoing marketing support and lead flow

For KMJK Home Improvement (Contractor):

  • ✅ Steady Volume - Consistent flow of quality projects
  • ✅ Maintain Your Margin - Keep your standard 30-40% profit margin

For The Elite Service Hub (Lead Provider):

  • ✅ Quality Execution - Professional contractor ensures customer satisfaction

Term and Termination

This Agreement shall remain in effect until terminated by either party with 30 days written notice. Both parties agree to complete all ongoing projects and fulfill payment obligations for leads provided prior to termination date.
